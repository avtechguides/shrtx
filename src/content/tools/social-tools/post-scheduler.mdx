# Social Media Post Scheduler

Plan and schedule social media posts directly from your browser without server dependencies.

<PostScheduler client:only="react"/>

## How it works

- Step 1: Connect your accounts.
- Step 2: Draft posts and set date/time.
- Step 3: Tool auto-posts on schedule.

## Features

- Multi-platform scheduling.
- Browser-only, offline-ready.
- Auto-post reminders and logs.

## Use cases

- Plan Instagram, TikTok, or Twitter campaigns.
- Maintain consistent posting schedule.
- Save time on repetitive content management.

## Tips

- Batch-upload posts for efficiency.
- Use templates for recurring content.
- Track scheduled posts with built-in logs.
