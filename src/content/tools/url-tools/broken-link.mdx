# Broken Link Checker

Scan URLs or pages quickly to find broken or dead links for SEO and maintenance.

<BrokenLinkChecker client:only="react" />

## How it works

- Step 1: Enter a URL or HTML content.
- Step 2: Click "Check" to detect broken links.
- Step 3: Review and fix any invalid URLs.

## Features

- Instant detection of broken links.
- Reports dead or invalid URLs.
- Works offline after first load.

## Use cases

- Maintain SEO-friendly websites.
- Ensure all links work before publishing.
- Check client websites or blogs for dead links.

## Tips

- Recheck periodically for new broken links.
- Use for website audits before updates.
- Share reports with developers for fixes.
