# QR Code Generator

Generate QR codes instantly for URLs, text, or contact info for quick sharing.

<QRCodeGenerator client:only="react" />

## How it works

- Step 1: Enter the data you want to encode.
- Step 2: Adjust size, colors, or format if needed.
- Step 3: Click "Generate QR" and copy or download the code.

## Features

- Instant browser processing.
- Download as PNG or SVG.
- Works offline after first load.

## Use cases

- Add QR codes to marketing materials.
- Share links in print media.
- Encode contact info for events or business cards.

## Tips

- Test scanning before sharing widely.
- Keep contrast high for better scanning.
- Use high-resolution for printing.
