# Time Zone Converter

Easily convert times between different time zones for meetings, events, or travel planning.

## How it works

- Step 1: Enter local time and date.
- Step 2: Select source and target time zones.
- Step 3: Get the converted time instantly.

## Features

- Supports all major global time zones.
- Automatically adjusts for daylight saving time.
- Works offline after first load.

## Use cases

- Schedule remote meetings easily.
- Convert time for flights or online events.
- Coordinate global teams and collaborators.

- Double-check date when converting across DST changes.
- Use with calendar apps for quick scheduling.
